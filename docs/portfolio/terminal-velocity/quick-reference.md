## When to Reference

**Strong signal in job post:**
- Keywords: multi-agent systems, autonomous AI, AI agents, content generation, creative AI, writing automation, long-form content, AI orchestration, agent coordination, KinOS
- Mentions: book writing, documentation generation, creative tools, narrative consistency, specialized agents, AI collaboration
- Client persona: AI research lab, content platform, publishing tech, edtech, creative tools startup, documentation automation

**Weak signal (don't force):**
- Single chatbot (not multi-agent)
- Short-form content only (tweets, product descriptions)
- Human-in-the-loop required for every step
- Real-time interaction (Terminal Velocity is batch/async)
