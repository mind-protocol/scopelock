## Risk Mitigation

### Risk: Low Win Rate (<10%)

**Symptoms:**
- Proposals viewed but not winning
- No interview requests

**Diagnosis:**
- Check proposal quality (too generic?)
- Check budget positioning (too high?)
- Check client targeting (wrong fit?)

**Solution:**
- A/B test proposal templates
- Lower bids temporarily (build testimonials)
- Narrow job selection criteria (higher quality leads)

---

### Risk: High Win Rate (>30%) But Low Revenue

**Symptoms:**
- Winning lots of jobs but all low-budget
- High volume, low profitability

**Diagnosis:**
- Targeting only $200-300 jobs
- Not bidding on higher-value missions

**Solution:**
- Shift to $400-800 tier missions
- Use testimonials to justify higher pricing
- Focus on AI integration (higher perceived value)

---

### Risk: Can't Keep Up With Won Jobs

**Symptoms:**
- Won 10 jobs, only 3 developers
- Delivery delays, quality issues

**Diagnosis:**
- Win rate too high for current capacity
- Need to slow down or hire

**Solution:**
- Reduce proposals temporarily (5/day â†’ 3/day)
- Increase prices (natural demand throttle)
- Hire 4th developer (per Alexis team management plan)

---

### Risk: Platform Saturation (Too Many Proposals, Low Visibility)

**Symptoms:**
- Proposals not even viewed by clients
- Jobs have 30+ proposals already

**Diagnosis:**
- Applying too late (jobs already have many bids)
- Not targeting niche enough

**Solution:**
- Apply within first 2 hours of job posting
- Use RSS alerts for instant notifications (Feature 4)
- Target more specialized missions (AI integration)
