## What Triggers Action (Critical Insights)

### 1. FOMO + Low Commitment
**Pattern:** He signs up for waitlist even though he's skeptical

**Why:**
- Signing up is FREE (no money down)
- Worst case: he's on a list and doesn't buy
- Best case: 10-20% discount + early access
- Can't lose by signing up

**Application for team:**
"Commit 4 hours this week. Worst case: you earn cash (30% pool). Best case: you earn cash + tokens worth $10K-$100K if token succeeds. Can't lose."

---

### 2. Quantified Edge
**Pattern:** He calculates exact scenarios ($400 → $4K at 10x)

**Why:**
- Needs to see the math (not just "you'll make money")
- Wants to compare to his past plays
- Edge must be quantifiable

**Application for team:**
"Competition: First to 10 proposals = +5,000 tokens. At $1.00 launch = $5,000. If token 10x to Bittensor levels = $50,000. Clear math, clear edge."

---

### 3. "Would Lie to Seem Serious"
**Pattern:** "Would put '$500-1000' commitment even if I have $87"

**Why:**
- Wants to be taken seriously
- Fears being excluded if commitment too low
- Willing to inflate intent to get access

**Application for team:**
**DON'T create this dynamic.** No "commitment amount" required. Just YES/NO to 4 hours/week. Keeps it honest.

---

### 4. Self-Aware About Lack of Discipline
**Pattern:** "Do I have discipline to sell at $5-10 before unlock? Probably not."

**Why:**
- He KNOWS he holds through dumps
- Acknowledges past mistakes
- Still signs up anyway (hope > experience)

**Application for team:**
This is retail behavior. Team members are likely more strategic. But STILL worth addressing:
"6-month lock gives you time to plan. Unlock at Month 6, price likely pumps before (anticipation), dumps after (unlock sell pressure). Strategy: sell 50% at unlock, hold 50% for long-term. We'll share unlock date in advance so you can plan."

---

### 5. Social Validation Check
**Pattern:** "Would go to X to search '$MIND', check if @DegenSpartan mentioned it, join Telegram"

**Why:**
- Needs social proof before committing money
- Wants to see if "smart people" are in
- Telegram = "insider" feeling

**Application for team:**
"You're on the TEAM Telegram. You're not waiting for influencers to tell you this is real. You're BUILDING the thing influencers will shill later. That's the founding team advantage."

## Summary: What To Use in Team Messaging

2. **"6-month lock = honest flexibility"** - Not fake 4-year vests
3. **"LP locked 12 months = verified rug protection"** - On-chain proof

3. **Token utility cycle** - Where buy pressure comes from (user funding → AI spending)

### ❌ Avoid These Red Flags
1. **NO commitment amount requirement** - Don't make them "prove" intent with numbers
2. **NO "you'll get rich" promises** - Realistic upside only
3. **NO pressure to invest cash** - Tokens earned, not bought
4. **NO hiding risks** - Transparent about "might not launch"
5. **NO emotional manipulation** - "Can't miss out" is data point, not threat

**His closing thought:** "This feels different. It feels REAL. But I've felt that before."
