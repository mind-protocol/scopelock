## Implementation Notes

### Sending Sequence
1. Copy CHUNK 1 → Paste and send
2. Wait 5-10 seconds
3. Copy CHUNK 2 → Paste and send
4. Wait 5-10 seconds
5. Copy CHUNK 3 → Paste and send
6. Wait 5-10 seconds
7. Copy CHUNK 4 → Paste and send

### Why This Works (Proof-First Framework)

**Old approach (template):**
- "Hey [name], saw you're working on..."
- Result: Triggers "burned founder" defensive mode

**New approach (specific):**
- "Saw your specific action/question [exact detail]"
- "Here's proof we solved this [live link]"
- "Here's why it matters [technical depth]"
- "Value offer [async, no pressure]"
- Result: Triggers curiosity instead of suspicion

### Conversion Expectations

Based on analysis document targets:
- Response rate target: >30% (vs. current ~10%)
- Technical filter test rate: >50% of responses
- Filter test pass rate: >80%
- Quality engagement: 3+ message exchanges

### Follow-Up Protocol

**If no response in 48h:**
- Send a *value* message, not a reminder
- Share one specific insight they'd find useful
- Still no pressure for response

**If they send technical filter question:**
- Answer within 2 hours with depth
- Show you understand their specific problem
- Share code/proof, not marketing

**If they engage:**
- Continue async (don't push for call)
- Let them ask for call if needed
- Stay in advisor mode, not sales mode
