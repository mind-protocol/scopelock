## Team Management Ideas

**2025-11-05 - Performance-Based Pay Increases**
- **Problem:** Flat $6-8/hour doesn't reward high performers
- **Idea:** Start at $6/hour, increase to $8 after 3 successful missions, $10 after 6 months if quality high
- **Impact:** Retention of best developers, motivation to deliver quality
- **Effort:** Low (just compensation decision)
- **Status:** New

**2025-11-05 - Lead Developer Role**
- **Problem:** Nicolas can't supervise 10+ junior devs alone
- **Idea:** Promote best performer to "lead dev" role (supervise 5 juniors, $15-20/hour)
- **Impact:** Scalability, mentorship, career growth for developers
- **Effort:** Medium (need to define role, responsibilities, training)
- **Status:** New (wait until Phase 3)

**2025-11-05 - Weekly Team Call (Optional)**
- **Problem:** Remote team may feel isolated
- **Idea:** Optional 30-min weekly team call (show what you built this week, Q&A)
- **Impact:** Team bonding, knowledge sharing, motivation
- **Effort:** Low (just calendar invite)
- **Status:** New
